Our Story

Inquisitors started in late 2023 when a group of CS students sitting in the cafeteria realized that memory retrieval in quizzing is super similar to machine learning algorithms.

We started with just 5 members meeting to solve pub trivia questions, and now we have over 80 active students from different majors across campus.

Club Goals

  • To build strong general knowledge and competitive quiz teams.
  • To help students get hands-on experience writing AI code.
  • To build open source tools that make studying easier.
