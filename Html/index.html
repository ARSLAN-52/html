Welcome!

Hi everyone, welcome to the Inquisitors Society website. We are a student-run club for people who like testing their knowledge in quizzes or tinkering with basic AI scripts.

Whether you want to join our competitive quiz team for university bowls or learn Python for machine learning, all skill levels are welcome.

What We Do

Trivia Practice

We meet up every Tuesday to practice general knowledge questions covering science, tech, history, pop culture, and sports.

Coding & AI Study Groups

We run peer study sessions on weekends teaching basic Python programming, API usage, and simple machine learning models.
